# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer data to identify churn patterns, understand customer retention trends, and provide actionable recommendations to reduce customer loss. The analysis helps businesses improve customer satisfaction and make data-driven decisions.

## Objective

* Identify customer churn patterns.
* Analyze factors affecting customer retention.
* Generate meaningful insights from customer data.
* Provide recommendations to improve customer loyalty.

## Tools Used

* Python
* Google Colab
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

## Data Preprocessing

* Checked for missing values.
* Verified duplicate records.
* Prepared and organized the dataset for analysis.

## Data Visualization

The following visualizations were created:

* Customer Churn Distribution
* Customer Churn by Gender
* Customer Churn by Contract Type
* Customer Churn by Payment Method
* Monthly Charges Distribution
* Customer Tenure vs Churn
* Customer Churn by Internet Service
* Correlation Heatmap

## Key Insights

* Month-to-month contract customers had the highest churn rate.
* Customers with shorter tenure were more likely to leave the service.
* Electronic check users showed higher churn rates.
* Higher monthly charges increased churn probability.
* Long-term customers demonstrated better retention and loyalty.

## Recommendations

* Encourage customers to choose long-term contracts.
* Provide loyalty rewards and retention offers.
* Improve customer support services.
* Target high-risk customers with personalized promotions.
* Monitor customer behavior regularly to reduce churn.

## Conclusion

The project successfully identified major factors influencing customer churn and provided valuable insights into customer retention behavior. The findings can help businesses reduce customer loss, improve retention strategies, and enhance overall business performance.
